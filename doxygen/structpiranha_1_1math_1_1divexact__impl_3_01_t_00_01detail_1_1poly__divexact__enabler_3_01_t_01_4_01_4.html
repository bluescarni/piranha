template<typename T>
struct piranha::math::divexact_impl< T, detail::poly_divexact_enabler< T > >

Implementation of piranha::math::divexact() for piranha::polynomial.

This specialisation is enabled if T is an instance of piranha::polynomial which supports exact polynomial division.
